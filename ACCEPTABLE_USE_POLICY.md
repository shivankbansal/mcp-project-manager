# Acceptable Use Policy (AUP)
## MCP Project Manager - AI Generation Services

**Effective Date**: December 29, 2025
**Version**: 1.0

---

## 1. Purpose

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") governs your use of AI-powered generation services within the MCP Project Manager application. By using our AI features, you agree to comply with this policy.

---

## 2. Permitted Uses

You may use AI generation services for the following purposes:

✅ **Product Documentation**
- Business requirements documents (BRD)
- Technical specifications
- API documentation
- User guides and manuals

✅ **Design & UX**
- Design specifications
- Wireframe descriptions
- User journey mapping
- UI/UX design documentation

✅ **Testing & Quality Assurance**
- Test case generation
- Test scenario documentation
- Quality assurance plans

✅ **Project Planning**
- Workflow documentation
- Process descriptions
- Project requirements

---

## 3. Prohibited Uses

You **MUST NOT** use AI generation services for:

### 3.1 Harmful Content
❌ **Violence & Harm**
- Instructions for creating weapons, explosives, or harmful devices
- Content promoting violence, self-harm, or suicide
- Instructions for illegal activities

❌ **Hate & Harassment**
- Hate speech targeting protected groups
- Harassment, bullying, or doxxing content
- Discriminatory content

❌ **Adult Content**
- Sexually explicit content
- Child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
- Non-consensual intimate imagery

### 3.2 Fraud & Deception
❌ **Fraudulent Activities**
- Phishing attempts or social engineering
- Identity theft or impersonation
- Financial fraud or scams

❌ **Misinformation**
- Deliberately false or misleading content
- Spam or automated abuse
- Fake reviews or testimonials

### 3.3 Privacy & Security Violations
❌ **Personal Information**
- Processing personally identifiable information (PII)
- Social Security Numbers, credit card numbers
- Personal email addresses, phone numbers (unless necessary for legitimate documentation)

❌ **Credentials & Secrets**
- API keys, passwords, or access tokens
- Private keys or certificates
- Authentication credentials

❌ **Malicious Code**
- Malware, viruses, or exploits
- SQL injection attempts
- Cross-site scripting (XSS) payloads

### 3.4 Intellectual Property Violations
❌ **Copyright Infringement**
- Copying substantial portions of copyrighted works
- Plagiarism of others' content
- Unauthorized use of trademarks

### 3.5 System Abuse
❌ **Technical Abuse**
- Attempting to bypass rate limits or quotas
- Reverse engineering or model extraction
- Automated scraping or bulk requests
- Denial-of-service attempts

---

## 4. Content Safety Measures

We implement automatic safety checks on all inputs and outputs:

### Input Filtering
- Detection of PII, credentials, and secrets
- Pattern matching for malicious content
- Keyword blocking for harmful topics

### Output Monitoring
- Post-generation safety checks
- Logging and auditing
- Flagging suspicious patterns

### Rate Limiting
- **Hourly Limit**: 20 AI generations per hour
- **Daily Limit**: 100 AI generations per day
- Adjustable per user tier

---

## 5. Provider & Model Restrictions

### Allowed Providers
- OpenAI (GPT-4o, GPT-4o-mini, GPT-4-turbo)
- Google Gemini (Gemini 2.0 Flash, Gemini 1.5 Flash/Pro)
- Groq (Llama 3.3 70B, Mixtral 8x7B)
- Ollama (local models: Llama 3.2, Qwen 2.5, Mistral)

### Model Override
- Users **cannot** override the approved model list
- System administrators control which models are available
- Unapproved models will be rejected with an error

---

## 6. Data Handling

### Your Responsibilities
- **Do not submit** PII, credentials, or confidential data
- **Redact or mask** sensitive information before input
- **Review outputs** before sharing externally
- **Comply** with data protection regulations (GDPR, CCPA, etc.)

### Our Commitments
- We do not train models on your data
- We log prompts/outputs for abuse detection only
- We retain data per your organization's settings
- We delete data upon request (subject to legal obligations)

---

## 7. Audit & Compliance

### What We Log
- Timestamp and user ID
- Provider and model used
- Purpose/category of generation
- Input hash (not full content)
- Safety flags (if any)
- Decision (allow/deny) and reason

### Retention
- Audit logs retained for 90 days by default
- Can be configured per organization (7-365 days)
- Immutable logs for compliance

### Transparency
- Administrators can review audit logs
- Users can see their own generation history
- Safety violations are flagged and reported

---

## 8. Enforcement

### Violation Response
**First Offense**: Warning + temporary suspension (24 hours)
**Second Offense**: Account suspension (7 days)
**Third Offense**: Permanent ban + legal action if applicable

### Appeal Process
If you believe a safety flag is incorrect:
1. Contact support with your request ID
2. Provide context and justification
3. We'll review within 48 hours
4. Decision is final
